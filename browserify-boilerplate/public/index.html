Chewsy

feast with your eyes / go with your gut

Chewsy is a single-page web application built with React, secured with Auth0, and styled with Reactstrap and CSS3. I built Chewsy to solve the modern day problem of big data and too many options getting in the way of honest restaurant choices. Instead of ratings and comments, users are presented with a simple picture of a dish and a yes or no button. Random images are presented based on a category query until an appetizing restaurant is chosen. At that point, the user is given information on the restaurant and the ability to save to their favorites. They may also follow other users and see what restaurants they have favorited.
